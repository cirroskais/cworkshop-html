CWorkshop

CWorkshop is a light, dynamic workshop downloader, meaning that workshop content is downloaded on-the-fly, when a client asks.

To download this server's content, click on the Addons tab at the top, and click Download All Addons. To reload your game's content, go to the Settings tab, click Reload Spawnmenu and then click Reload Effects. You should now be able to see most of the downloaded content.

Finally, thank you for using my addon! If you encounter any bugs, please do not hesitate to report them on GitHub.
